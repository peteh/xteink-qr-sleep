How to use

  1. Select your device above
  2. Fill in your contact details
  3. Download the PNG file
  4. Copy it into .sleep/ on your SD card
  5. In CrossInk: Settings → Display → Sleep Screen → Page overlay

CrossInk's Page Overlay mode makes white areas transparent — your book page shows through, the QR code floats on top.
